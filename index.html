ONE FINANCIAL PICTURE

Designed around the decisions you make every month.

UpTier connects the numbers that are usually scattered across broker exports, statements, spreadsheets, and calendars.

01

Portfolio clarity

Import and reconcile broker CSV files, track invested value, review allocation, and keep ambiguous rows from silently duplicating holdings.

02

Dividend intelligence

Use confirmed payment history to build monthly or quarterly schedules, project the next 12 months, and model optional dividend reinvestment.

03

Budget foresight

See actual upcoming pay periods, recurring bills, debts, and the lowest projected cash point across the next 30 days.

04

A useful calendar

Bring estimated dividends, confirmed payouts, income, bills, and debt payments together without turning the calendar into an activity log.

05

Local profile vault

Keep financial profiles encrypted on the device, with each profile isolated and its key protected for the current Windows user.

06

Progress that matters

Measure invested capital, passive income, milestones, and financial-freedom progress instead of reacting to daily market noise.

OPTIONAL CONNECTED PORTFOLIO

A user-controlled path from Plaid to UpTier.

UpTier's Plaid Investments integration is being validated with Sandbox data and a limited, invitation-only real-data Trial. It will not be generally offered until provider review, security, and release requirements are complete.

Sandbox and private Trial under validation
  1. 1

    You choose to connect

    UpTier opens Plaid's hosted connection experience. The desktop app never asks for or stores brokerage login credentials.

  2. 2

    The backend normalizes supported data

    A secure UpTier backend requests authorized investment holdings and supported transaction history. Plaid secrets and access tokens stay off the desktop.

  3. 3

    You import into your local profile

    UpTier reconciles supported positions and investment-income history into the encrypted profile on your device for review and planning.

DATA PRINCIPLES

Your information should have a clear purpose.

Local by default

Portfolio, budget, calendar, and planning data stay in the local encrypted profile unless you deliberately use an optional provider.

No advertising profile

The current Windows app has no behavioral advertising, third-party analytics, or automatic crash-report uploads.

No sale of personal data

UpTier does not sell personal information. Optional providers receive only the information needed for the feature you choose to use.

Documented deletion path

Local and connected-data removal instructions are public and available without signing in.
